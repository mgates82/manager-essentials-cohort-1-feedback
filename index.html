💡 What's Working
Small group breakout sessions are consistently praised as the most impactful element across all three modules.
Practical frameworks (Eisenhower Matrix, Tuckman, DACI) resonate strongly — participants appreciate tools they can apply immediately.
Supporting artifacts and study guides are highly rated — participants feel well-supported with take-home materials.
Likelihood to practice scores are high and increasing across modules (4.36 → 4.42 → 4.67), suggesting growing engagement.
🔧 Recurring Improvements
Breakout / role-play time is consistently cited as too short — participants want more practice time, not less discussion.
Requests for more cross-functional application guidance — how do these tools apply beyond direct reports?
A few participants missed sessions and note the need for recordings as a fallback.
Prework timing: some participants complete it too close to the session to fully process the material beforehand.
